AM I ELIGIBLE TO USE PRIME RIDES SERVICES?

To avail Prime Rides services, you must be at least 21 years old, and you should hold valid driving license for Light Motor Vehicles.

WHICH ARE THE AREAS WHERE I CAN AVAIL PRIME RIDES SERVICES?

Delhi NCR : We are covering Delhi, Gurgaon, Faridabad, Noida & Ghaziabad.

ARE THERE ANY RESTRICTED PLACES FOR TRAVEL?

You can drive our cars entire India however we do not permit taking Prime Rides vehicles to Leh/Ladakh region, Kaza/Nako region and spit valley. We also advise you to avoid bad terrains (especially in non- SUV cars) and areas restricted by local bodies and goverment.

DO I HAVE TO BRING THE CAR BACK TO THE SAME LOCATION?

You can choose multiple locations across Delhi NCR for pick up and delivery. Standard charges will be applied according to distance.

CAN I BOOK A ONE-WAY TRIP WITH PRIME RIDES?

Currently this facility in not available.

CAN I BOOK FOR ANY DURATION OF TIME?

For rentals, the minimum booking duration is 12 hours, and the maximum is up to 180 days.
If you need a car for longer period of time, please try Prime Rides subscriptions, wherein you can subscribe to a car for minimum of 1 month and maximum of 36 months.

IS PEAK SEASON? ARE THE PRICES DIFFERENT DURING PEAK SEASON?

Peak season refers to festive periods of very high demand.

IN THE EVENT OF AN ACCIDENT OR DAMAGE TO THE VEHICLE, WHAT IS MY MAXIMUM LIABILITY?

  1. In the event of damages, Your liability is limited to a maximum of Rs 100000.
    You will need to pay Damage Fee equivalent to the repair cost (as estimated by an authorized dealership) and downtime losses, upto a maximum of Rs 100000.
    This is independent of the nature and terms of the insurance cover that has on the cars.
  2. Please note that the maximum liability policy is intended to benefit and protect the responsible user. In situations of irresponsible or unsafe or negligent driving, there could be discretionary exceptions to this policy, whereby the user could be liable for all damages irrespective of whether or not Prime Rides is able to claim insurance in such cases. Examples of such situation include,
    But are not limited to:-
    • When the User is not in compliance with the laws and/or with terms of the Prime Rides User Agreement
    • When the User is found to be under the influence of alcohol/narcotics while driving
    • When the User is over-speeding (i.e. above prescribed speed limit of road or 120 Kms/Hr, whichever is lower)
    • When the User is in violation of traffic rules or of the Motor Vehicles Act (for example, if the User has not paid the inter-state tax while crossing a state border)
    • When the person driving the vehicle at the time of the incident is found to be different than the one who made the booking
    • When the vehicle workshop and/or insurance company deem the damage to be consequential in nature (this will be clearly stated in the accident report). Consequential damage occurs when the User continues driving the vehicle even when it is performing abnormally
    • When the number of people in the car at the time of the incident exceed the standard seating capacity of the car
    • When the incident happened in a state for which the user had failed to pay the inter-state tax while entering the state
    • When there is misrepresentation of information in the driving license and ID proof details shared by the user
    • Where there is clear evidence of extremely rash and negligent driving on the user’s part
  3. In cases where the estimated damage charges exceed the security deposit, we will request the user to pay the same at the time of returning the car.
    In cases of non-payment, Prime Rides reserves the right of legal action, reporting to credit bureau and reporting to other authorities or persons, irrespective of their direct relevance to the case.
  4. In cases of accident, fare for the unused hours will not be refunded.
  5. Prime Rides typically avoids charging for very minor damages (e.g., minor scratches and dents), and covers the vast majority of damage incidents under the maximum liability policy.
  6. In cases of collision or User failure, towing and impounding charges will be paid by the User. In cases of vehicle failure, towing and impounding charges will be paid by us.
